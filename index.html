How does it work?

Step one
We’ll send you jobs that we think your friends can do. You refer the best candidates.
Step two
If your friend gets the job, you get the cash! You’ll earn a referral fee of at least $1000.
Step three
Build a team of headhunters. For every referral they make, we pay you 7.5%.
